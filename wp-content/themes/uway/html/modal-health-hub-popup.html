Celery Juice

Ingredients:

½ bunch of organic celery, leaves and all

Method:

  • Wash celery, trim of any yellowing leaves, and any leaves that have black spot.
  • Using a cold press juicer, juice the celery, tops and all.
  • Drink immediately
